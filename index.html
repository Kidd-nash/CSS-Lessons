Love letter

You cannot grasp water, but it is inside a soil You cannot touch fire, but it is inside a tree You cannot hold wind, but it is resting on the rock If you let them be, they will exist everywhere

The faint blue light, makes me sigh My white breath, turned into snow and tears I could not let them drop and hid them Despite knowing that they will fill up once again

I am a fool who dares not shed a single tear
